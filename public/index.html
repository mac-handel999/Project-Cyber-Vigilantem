🔍 Who Is (Domain & IP) Scanner

WHOIS is a standard network protocol used to look up the public registration data and ownership "birth certificate" of a domain name or IP address. In cybersecurity operations, analysts use it as an essential OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence) reconnaissance tool to audit network infrastructure allocations, verify domain age to detect new phishing threats, track expiration timelines, and assess SSL certificate status parameters.
